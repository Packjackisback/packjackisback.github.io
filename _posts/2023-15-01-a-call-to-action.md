My school district has decided it would be a good idea to ban students' personal devices, ignoring the obvious *very* negative reprocussions of that. For one, extra-curriculars are near impossible now, as cordinating pickups during the school day is only possible through email addresses, which many guardians either don't have, or don't check. For a district that seems to pride itself on only the extra-curriculars, this is very troubling. I think this really reflects the intent of the board members, which really only were elected onto the board because of the [republican takeover](https://www.npr.org/2023/01/10/1148211343/a-coordinated-nationwide-agenda-dominated-local-school-board-elections-last-year) of our school board. The board clearly has only joined because of the lies told by the [Republican party](https://nymag.com/intelligencer/article/desantis-florida-trump-education-politics.html).
